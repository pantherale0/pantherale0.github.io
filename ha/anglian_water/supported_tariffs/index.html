    Supported Tariffs

    This integration supports several Anglian Water tariffs, allowing for accurate cost estimations. When configuring the integration, you will need to select the tariff that applies to your account. Here's a breakdown of the supported tariffs:

    Anglian Area

    Tariff Rate (£ per cubic meter) Standing Charge (£ per year) Notes
    Standard 2.0954 37.00
    LITE 1.5716 27.75
    AquaCare Plus 1.0087 118.50
    Extra LITE 1.0477 18.50
    LITE 60 0.8382 14.80
    LITE 80 0.4191 7.40
    WaterSure 241.00 N/A Charged annually
    Custom 0.00 N/A Requires manual rate configuration

    Hartlepool Area

    Tariff Rate (£ per cubic meter) Standing Charge (£ per year) Notes

    LITE 60 0.4878 12.60

    Tariff Rate (£ per cubic meter) Standing Charge (£ per year) Notes
    Everyday 1.1053 47.28
    WaterSure 164.69 N/A Charged annually
    LITE 0.8290 35.45
    Extra LITE 0.5527 23.60
    Custom 0.00 N/A Requires manual rate configuration

    Finningley Area

    This area currently only supports a custom tariff:

    Tariff Rate (£ per cubic meter) Notes
    Custom 0.00 Requires manual rate configuration

    Woods Meadow Area

    This area currently does not have any defined tariffs.

    Notes

    • The "Rate" is the cost per cubic meter of water used.
    • The "Standing Charge" is a fixed annual charge.
    • "WaterSure" tariffs are charged annually, not per cubic meter.
    • "Custom" tariffs require you to manually enter the rate in the integration's configuration. This is useful if your tariff is not listed or if it changes.

    This information is based on the currently available data within the integration. Tariff details may change, so it's always best to check your latest bill from Anglian Water for the most accurate information.
